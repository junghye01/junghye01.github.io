About Me

I'm a M.S. student at Korea University's AGILAB, currently advised by Prof. Sungwoong Kim and Prof. Sungbin Lim.

I received my B.S. from Dongguk University, where I double-majored in Information & Communication Engineering and Data Science.

My research interests lie in multimodal large language models, particularly unified models for video understanding and generation. I aim to enhance semantic alignment and model reliability to advance intelligent video-based AI systems.
