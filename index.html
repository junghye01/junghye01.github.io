About Me

I'm a M.S. student at Korea University's AGILAB, currently advised by Prof. Sungwoong Kim and Prof. Sungbin Lim.

My research interests lie in video understanding and multimodal reasoning, with a focus on enabling AI systems to understand complex real-world scenarios as humans do. I am particularly interested in modeling fine-grained human-object interactions, temporal dynamics, and causal relationships in videos to develop reliable video understanding systems.
